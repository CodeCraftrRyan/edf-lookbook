Create an EDF Lookbook

Simply upload a CSV file and click “Generate Lookbook.” A PDF will automatically be downloaded to your desktop.

To ensure the tool works correctly, your CSV file should include any or all of the following column headers in lowercase:

  • "Headshot" Note: Headshot images are taken directly from Salesforce and should be formatted as first_last1.jpg
  • "Name"
  • "Company"
  • "Title"
  • "Bio"
